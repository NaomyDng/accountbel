ABOUT ACCOUNT-BEL

Account-Bel is a fiduciary firm recognized by the I.T.A.A, registered under number 50.593.883 and founded in 2004. The firm was created by Michaël SPANOUDIS, a chartered accountant since 1996, with a clear ambition: to provide demanding, human, and tailored accounting support to both entrepreneurs and individuals.

At Account-Bel, we don’t make empty promises. We get the job done seriously, rigorously with a client-focused, transparent approach. Here, every client is seen as a partner, and every file as a personal commitment.

Whether you are self-employed, a company director, in transition, or a private individual, you deserve a firm that understands your challenges and guides you clearly and honestly.

Surrounding Michaël SPANOUDIS is a team of experts with complementary profiles who manage your accounting with precision, confidentiality, and efficiency.

Our mission: to help you move forward with confidence, because when the numbers are clear, so is your mind.
